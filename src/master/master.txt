The program is mainly composed by 4 functions: init(), accepter(), receiver(), flush_show().
-init() for initialize the socket. 
-accepter() is the function of a thread which will create a list of all the client.
-receiver() will collect all the messages of drones: position,fuel, state, name.
-flush_show() will print and refresh the arena in a precis frequency.
